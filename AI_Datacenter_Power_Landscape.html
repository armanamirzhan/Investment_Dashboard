Critical Constraint: HALEU Fuel Supply — Centrus Energy (LEU) Report →

High-Assay Low-Enriched Uranium (HALEU) is required by most advanced reactors (Oklo, X-energy, Kairos). Centrus Energy (LEU) is the only US commercial HALEU producer. Revenue $449M, net income $78M (profitable). DOE contract extended through June 2026 (~$110M). HALEU market projected to grow from $0.26B to $6.2B by 2030. Analyst consensus: Buy, avg target $265.

Key Risk Factors & Supply Chain Bottlenecks

Grid Interconnection Queue

Severity: Critical. US queue at 2,600 GW backlog. Median wait: ~5 years, some projects facing 12-year delays. PJM and ERCOT most constrained.

Impact: Primary reason hyperscalers are pursuing behind-the-meter (BTM) solutions. ExxonMobil, Enchanted Rock, Solaris bypassing grid entirely.

Source: RMI, Utility Dive, ENR

Power Transformer Shortage

Severity: Critical. 30% supply deficit for power transformers in 2025. Lead times: 128 weeks (power), 144 weeks (GSU). Prices up 60-70% since 2020.

Mitigation: Eaton ($340M SC plant by 2027), Siemens ($150M NC plant by 2027), Hitachi expanding VA/PA. Deficit expected to ease by 2030.

Source: Wood Mackenzie, POWER Magazine

Gas Turbine Backlog

Severity: Critical. GE Vernova sold out through 2030. Siemens backlog extends 5+ years. Mitsubishi doubling manufacturing but still catching up.

Impact: Hyperscalers using older aeroderivative models as bridge power. Order lead times pushing projects to 2028+.

Source: GE Vernova, Siemens Energy earnings

HALEU Fuel Scarcity

Severity: Medium. Required for most SMRs (Oklo, X-energy, Kairos). Centrus (LEU) only commercial US source. Could delay SMR deployments beyond 2030.

Source: DOE, NRC filings

Regulatory & Permitting Risk

Severity: Medium. Ireland, Netherlands blocking new DC connections. Parts of Virginia restricting growth. Pennsylvania governor threatened grid withdrawal. NRC reviews for SMRs taking 18+ months.

Source: MultiState, industry reports

Hyperscaler Demand Pullback

Severity: Medium. Microsoft froze 1.5 GW self-build and cancelled 2 GW leases. Only ~5 GW of promised 16 GW for 2026 actually being built. AI inference efficiency gains could reduce power needs.

Mitigation: Overall CapEx still rising. Demand acceleration from Goldman Sachs revised up to 220% growth by 2030.

Source: SemiAnalysis, Benzinga

📊 Wall St (Analyst Consensus) Yahoo Finance · refreshed hourly

📈 Upside (Analyst Price Target) Yahoo Finance · refreshed hourly

Implied percentage upside (or downside) to the consensus 12-month price target, computed as: (target ÷ current price − 1) × 100. Positive values mean analysts collectively see room to run; negative values mean the stock is trading above where they expect it in 12 months.

Useful as a quick read on how stretched a stock is versus where the Street thinks it should be — but note that targets lag fundamentals and tend to anchor near current prices for widely-covered names.

Source: Yahoo Finance via yfinance (targetMeanPrice ÷ currentPrice)
