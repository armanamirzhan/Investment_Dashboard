## KEY OBSERVATIONS

1. **AI Model Developer Consolidation**: OpenAI, Anthropic, and xAI are dominant in North America; Mistral leads in Europe; DeepSeek in Asia. Chinese players (Alibaba, Baidu, Tencent, Zhipu) have significant embedded AI.

2. **Enterprise Platform Shift**: Salesforce, ServiceNow, Snowflake, and Databricks are heavily monetizing AI. Palantir is the pure-play AI analytics leader for enterprise/government.

3. **Infrastructure Software Gap**: Hugging Face, Weights & Biases, Scale AI, and Replicate are solving real MLOps/infrastructure problems with strong unit economics.

4. **Inference Boom**: Fireworks AI, Groq, Together AI, and Modal are addressing the $100B+ inference opportunity. This is one of the most competitive new categories.

5. **EDA Oligopoly**: Synopsys and Cadence dominate (with 70%+ share), but their AI-powered tools are critical to chip design infrastructure. Less disruption here.

6. **Vertical AI Emergence**: Tempus, Harvey, Jasper, and Replit show strong vertical application demand. These companies have defensible business models.

7. **Security & Governance Underfunded**: Robust Intelligence, Arthur AI, and Fiddler Labs address critical needs but are early-stage. This category could see significant consolidation.

8. **Private Company Valuation Spike**: 2023-2024 saw massive increases for leading AI infrastructure plays ($1B+ for Hugging Face, Scale AI, Weights & Biases).
