   Implication: Diversified monetization reduces risk. Pure plays highest risk.

4. VALUATION HIERARCHY
   - MSFT (24.1x) trades lowest despite leadership position (justified: proven monetization)
   - META (22.6x) trades low relative to growth (valuation opportunity)
   - ORCL (31.3x) trades high relative to maturity (execution risk priced in)

   Implication: Valuation not uniformly expensive, but all prices in AI success.

5. FINANCING INNOVATION MATTERS
   - Oracle's BYOH model transfers GPU supply/financial risk to customers
   - CoreWeave's customer prepayments reduce direct capital requirements
   - Microsoft/Amazon/Google: Absorbing full capex directly into balance sheet
