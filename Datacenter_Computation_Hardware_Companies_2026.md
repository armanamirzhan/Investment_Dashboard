## KEY INDUSTRY TRENDS (2026)

1. **AI Factories**: Multiple data centers connected via optical links, behaving as single supercomputers
2. **Custom ASICs**: Cloud providers (Google, Meta, AWS, Microsoft, Alibaba) reducing NVIDIA dependency
3. **Liquid Cooling**: Mandatory for >250kW/rack power densities; immersion gaining share
4. **Memory Constraints**: HBM capacity-constrained; 2026 = transition year to HBM4
5. **Optical Boom**: 800G/1.6T/3.2T transceiver volume exploding (37M units 800G in 2026 alone)
6. **Thermal Limits**: Rack power density hitting 50-100kW challenges traditional cooling
7. **Vertical Integration**: Hyperscalers designing own chips, servers, and systems
8. **China Push**: Alibaba, other Chinese firms developing domestic alternatives
9. **Consolidation**: Major M&A (Groq→NVIDIA, Nokia→Infinera, Amphenol→Smiths, etc.)
10. **Inference Focus**: Shift from training to inference hardware optimization
