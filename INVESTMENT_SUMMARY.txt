INVESTMENT THESIS SUMMARY
================================================================================

🟢 NVIDIA (NVDA) — STRONG BUY
   Thesis: Dominant GPU market leader with 88% AI/DC exposure, CUDA moat, 24x fwd P/E
           with 69% growth = exceptional value. Blackwell ramp and inference growth
           catalysts through 2027-2028.
   Risk:   Customer concentration, China export restrictions, competition from AMD/custom
   Action: Accumulate on weakness below $180; core long-term holding

🟡 AMD (AMD) — BUY
   Thesis: Strong second-place position with EPYC server CPU dominance (+39% DC growth)
           and MI450 inflection in 2H 2026. Dual revenue streams reduce risk vs. NVDA.
   Risk:   Execution on MI350/MI450; NVIDIA's 3-year software lead; 29x P/E valuation
   Action: Buy on pullbacks to $200; 3-5 year holding; monitor quarterly execution

🟠 BROADCOM (AVGO) — HOLD
   Thesis: End-to-end AI infrastructure provider with $73B backlog and path to $100B+
           annual AI revenue by 2027. 68% EBITDA margins exceptional.
   Risk:   Valuation stretched at 30x fwd P/E; backlog quality uncertain; concentrated
           customer base; 140% YoY growth hard to sustain
   Action: Hold existing; avoid new positions above $400; buy on 15%+ weakness

🔴 INTEL (INTC) — SELL/AVOID
   Thesis: Broken turnaround play. 50x fwd P/E for -2% revenue guidance unjustifiable.
           Gaudi GPU adoption anemic; 3+ years behind in AI. Server CPU upside temporary.
   Risk:   Dividend cut likely 18mo; IFS capital intensive; foundry viability questionable
   Action: Avoid initiating; exit on any rallies above $65; do not hold

🟡 TSMC (TSM) — BUY
   Thesis: Indispensable foundry with 2-3 year technology lead, 61% AI/HPC revenue mix,
           54% gross margins. 24.6x P/E offers best risk-adjusted return in complex.
   Risk:   Geopolitical Taiwan risk; customer concentration (NVIDIA 50%+); capacity cycle
   Action: Core holding; accumulate at any price below $400; 5-7 year horizon recommended
