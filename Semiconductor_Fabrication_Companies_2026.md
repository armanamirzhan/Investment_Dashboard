## KEY INVESTMENT THEMES 2026

1. **AI Supply Chain Bottleneck**: CoWoS, HBM, and advanced packaging are heavily capacity-constrained
2. **CHIPS Act Implementation**: U.S. fab buildouts driving equipment and material demand
3. **China Consolidation**: SMIC/Hua Hong mergers reshaping foundry competitive landscape
4. **SiC/GaN Acceleration**: Power electronics for EVs and AI infrastructure ramping
5. **EUV Scaling**: ASML maintaining monopoly; lithography capex remains elevated
6. **Photoresist Leadership**: Japan (TOK, JSR, Shin-Etsu) controls 95% of EUV resist market
7. **Vertical Integration**: Major equipment suppliers acquiring consumables producers (Entegris/CMC)
8. **CoWoS Capacity Race**: TSMC expanding 3.7x by end of 2026; OSATs developing alternatives
