Business Overview & DC Power Thesis

Core thesis: Schneider is the global leader in energy management with the broadest product portfolio for datacenter power — from medium-voltage switchgear to UPS to intelligent PDUs. Its EcoStruxure platform provides software-defined energy management that hyperscalers increasingly demand.

Investment Attractiveness — Pros & Cons

Bull Case

  • Broadest DC power product portfolio globally
  • 18.7% EBITA margin with 50-80bp expansion guided
  • €4.6B FCF with 111% conversion rate
  • Strong dividend growth (8% YoY)
  • 18% DC switchgear market share — #1 position

Bear Case

  • European-listed, less accessible for US investors
  • Broad industrial conglomerate — DC is part of larger business
  • Forward P/E of 28x not cheap for diversified industrial
  • Currency risk (EUR-denominated)
