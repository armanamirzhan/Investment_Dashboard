Intel +9-10% premarket on report Google ordered 3M+ TPUs from Intel Foundry on 18A; NVDA also evaluating 18A for Feynman — Intel surged 9-10% premarket Tuesday after multiple outlets reported Google has placed an order for Intel Foundry to manufacture more than 3 million in-house TPUs on the 18A process node for 2028 production, potentially scaling to 6M+ units across 2027-28 depending on yield. The deal — the largest external-customer validation of 18A to date — also coincides with separate reports that NVIDIA is running early trials of 18A and evaluating complex multi-chip designs for the post-Rubin 'Feynman' platform. The news adds to a multi-day PT-raise wave on INTC: Wells Fargo $110 (from $85), Barclays $100 (from $65), Mizuho $128. Read-through is incrementally negative for TSMC at the margin given hyperscaler appetite for second-source capacity, though TSM still holds the lead-node monopoly on Blackwell/Rubin. The order also reflects TSMC capacity tightness as datacenter demand outstrips foundry buildout.
