AI Datacenter Exposure

Product Portfolio & Market Position

Coherent is the dominant supplier of optical components for AI datacenter interconnect:

  • 800G/1.6T Transceivers: Next-generation optical transceivers driving AI cluster interconnects with 400G/lane capability
  • Indium Phosphide (InP) Dominance: Only 6-inch InP supplier in production, with Sherman and Jarfalla fabs ramping capacity for transceiver demand
  • Co-Packaged Optics (CPO): Advanced CPO architectures reducing latency and power consumption in AI data center networks
  • 3.2T & 12.8T+ Systems: Breakthrough innovations for next-generation AI cluster topologies
  • Datacenter Revenue Growth: +60% YoY in FY2025, representing majority of growth
  • Communications Segment: Double-digit sequential and YoY growth in Q4 FY2025, supporting hyperscaler build-out

Market Drivers: Every new AI cluster requires significant optical networking upgrades. With 800G becoming baseline and 1.6T emerging, Coherent's optical transceiver content per data center is rising substantially.

Growth Catalysts

  • 800G Mainstream Adoption: Rapid transition from 400G to 800G architecture across hyperscalers (Meta, MSFT, GOOG, AMZN)
  • 1.6T Transceiver Ramp: Next-generation 1.6T optical transceivers entering production, supporting ultra-dense AI cluster builds
  • InP Capacity Expansion: Sherman and Jarfalla fab utilization ramping to meet multi-year transceiver demand surge
  • CPO Adoption Inflection: Co-packaged optics becoming preferred architecture for new hyperscaler data center builds
  • NVIDIA Ecosystem Growth: H100, H200, and next-gen GPU clusters driving sustained demand for optical interconnect
  • Open RAN Opportunities: Expanding addressable market in telecom 5G/6G infrastructure optical components
  • Multiple Margin Expansion Paths: Mix shift to higher-margin 1.6T products and manufacturing efficiency gains

Risk Factors

  • Hyperscaler Concentration: Revenue heavily dependent on Meta, Microsoft, Google, Amazon investment levels
  • Technology Transition Risk: Potential disruption from silicon photonics or alternative transceiver architectures
  • Supply Chain Bottlenecks: InP wafer availability could constrain growth if capacity lags demand
  • Competitive Intensity: New entrants in optical transceiver market, particularly from Asian competitors
  • Capital Intensity: Significant capex required to expand InP manufacturing capacity
  • Valuation Risk: Stock trading at significant premium to historical averages; growth must sustain to justify multiples
  • Geopolitical Exposure: China restrictions on advanced semiconductor equipment could impact operations
  • Macro Cyclicality: Data center investment cycles could slow if economic conditions deteriorate

Investment Thesis

Why Coherent Merits Strong Buy Rating

Coherent Corp represents a high-conviction AI infrastructure play with structural growth visibility through 2028. The company operates as the near-monopoly supplier of next-generation optical transceivers that enable AI datacenter interconnect at scale. This positioning is nearly impossible to replicate due to its unique Indium Phosphide manufacturing expertise and capacity advantages.

Key Investment Highlights:

  • Dominant market share in 800G and emerging 1.6T optical transceiver segment with limited competition
  • 60% datacenter revenue growth in FY2025 with accelerating trajectory as hyperscalers deploy new AI clusters
  • Revenue growth of 43% CAGR through 2028E ($5.81B to $10.6B), implying $4.8B incremental revenue opportunity
  • Gross margin expansion story with 358 bps improvement in FY2025, targeting 40%+ as volume ramps
  • EPS CAGR of 45% through 2028E driven by revenue growth and operating leverage
  • Diversified end-market exposure beyond hyperscalers (telecom, aerospace, industrial)

Valuation: While trading at 42.8x FY2025 non-GAAP P/E, the 43% revenue CAGR implies a PEG ratio of 0.99x, indicating valuation is reasonable relative to growth profile. Consensus target of $248.33 offers 18.5% upside with limited downside if guidance maintained.

Catalysts: Near-term catalysts include 800G mainstream adoption announcements, 1.6T production ramp commentary in quarterly calls, and potential guidance raises as hyperscaler capex guides remain elevated for 2026-2027.

Conviction: Strong Buy rating reflects high-quality growth business at reasonable valuation with clear multi-year visibility. Suitable for growth-oriented portfolios with 2-3 year investment horizon.
